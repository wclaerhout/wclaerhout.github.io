About this blog

evo blog is a learning journal. I'm teaching myself to be a better Julia programmer by building things: implementing algorithms from scratch, reading papers and coding up the ideas, and generally spending time at the intersection of math and working code.

Every post is backed by a Pluto.jl notebook. Pluto's reactivity means I can share interactive explorations — change a parameter, watch the plot update. The notebooks live on GitHub; links are in each post.

Topics I keep coming back to: numerical methods, optimisation, signal processing, and occasionally paper implementations when something in the ML literature catches my eye.
